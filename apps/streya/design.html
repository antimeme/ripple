Streya Design

Streya is a systemic web game set in the Kuiper belt. It attempts to strike a balance between realism and flexibility. This means players must be able to understand things at a high level without detailed knowledge of the underlying systems. Carefully managing lower level systems must create advantages but not usually decisive ones. This is meant to support a collection of interconnected games with emphasis on different mechanics.

Systems

These are the major systems that make up Streya.

  • Structures: places for character to inhabit and manage systems
  • Editor: specifies desired structure configurations
  • Navigation: guides ships to destinations based on deltaV
  • Mining: collects resources from natural bodies
  • Mechanics: keeps engines and other ship systems operating
  • Shields: optimize resources to deflect external attacks
  • Gunnery: coordinate attacks on enemy structures
  • Drone Control: coordinate and maintain robots
  • Research: explore design space to improve facilities
  • Engineering: coordinate manufacturing systems

Characters

Characters generally occupy structures and make use of their facilities.

  • Attributes: trade offs that confer fundamental advantages and disadvantages
  • Face: distinctive appearance of a character
  • Background: where the character has been in the past
  • Skills: knowledge and experience that improves performance
  • Needs: things required to maintain health
  • Inventory: items worn and carried

Combat: Character

Roles

  • Retreat: pull back with minimum casualties
  • Hold: prevent an enemy from advancing into an area
  • Advance: attempt to clear an area of enemy combatants
  • Attack: focus fire on a specific target
